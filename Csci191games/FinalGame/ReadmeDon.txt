1. On the landing page, click the left mouse button or press enter to go to the Menu

On the Menu Page
2. Click or Press "N" to start a new game
3. Click or Press "H" for Help
4. Click or Press "E" to exit the game

During play state
5. Press "ESC" to pause the game and offer to Exit by pressing "Enter" 
   or to Resume the game by pressing "ESC"
